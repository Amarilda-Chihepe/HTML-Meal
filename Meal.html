Bean stew

Origin

Bean stew is one of the most well-known and popular typical dishes of Brazilian cuisine. Composed basically of black beans, various parts of the pig, sausage, flour and the accompaniment of vegetables and vegetables, it is commonly pointed out as a culinary creation of enslaved Africans who came to Brazil.
